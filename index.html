The studio

Evidence Studio

Independent design practice focused on identity & branding.

Evidence Studio is an independent graphic design and branding practice. We work at the intersection of strategy and form — building identities that are deliberate, rigorous, and enduring.

We believe great design leaves a trace. Every mark, every decision is evidence of intention. Our process begins with understanding how a brand thinks, then translating that into a visual system that can hold its own over time.

We collaborate with founders, cultural institutions, and companies who understand that design is not decoration — it is argument.
